Murena is an umbrella name for the /e/OS operating system, associated open source powered smartphones, and cloud-based services. The Murena project provides open source images for common smartphones, open source cloud-based storage, calendar, and backup solutions, and sells phones with /e/OS pre-installed. The project's operating system is based on LineageOS, itself based on Android. The Murena team removes closed source applications, trackers, and Google-specific elements of Android and replaces them with open source alternatives.

 Hi, I started e/OS as an experiment to reuse my daughters old sony-smartphone (Model I3113), after she stopped using it, due to heavy battery usage, and she bougt herself a new smartphone. After about 3 weeks of testing different apps and updates, I switched my dayly usage smartphone, and now this e/OS smartphone is my dayly companion for more than 9 months. The operating systems works reliably, and due to the minimal background prozesses this old smartphone only needs a battery-reload about 2 times per week. ... I find it VERY positive that I do not need a google-account or a murena-account to get software-updates, and can use the full playstore (even online-banking app works without any problems, it is only necessary to grant that one app more security-rights to work ... every other app does not need that much rights) ...

I am very happy to have found this OS by accident, because it is a reliable alternative to google and apple
