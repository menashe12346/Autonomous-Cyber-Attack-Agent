 I switched to Iodeos about a year ago after purchasing a new old stock Pixel 5 phone. The OS was installed just after I opened the box.
I had been trying to escape Big Tech for some time.

The system installed faultlessly and I have not had a hitch since. Battery consumption is excellent and my monthly data consumtion has reduced quite dramatically despite spending more time online.

I have not bothered with Aurora store, just sticking with FDroid, nor micro G services so I remain some distance from Big Brother.
The only additions to the preinstalled apps are Open Camera which I prefer to Apperture, Molly(FOSS) which I find to be a perfect fit for my needs,Tuta Mail and Proton VPN both of which I subscribe anyway. The phone functions smoothly at all times.

My partner was so impressed that when her ancient ZTE phone had battery issues I purchased another new old stock Pixel 5 and she couldnt be happier.

It continues to amuse me greatly that whenever I turn the phone on it comes up with the Google logo before transforming into IodeOS.


I would recommend IodeOS to anybody and there is a reasonable selection of compatible devices to choose from. Just look around for a suitable device as new old stock, install IodeOS and off you go!!!
